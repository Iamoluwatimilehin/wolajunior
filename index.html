INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Children and Teenagers Church of Word of life Assembly where the love of God is abundant and dreams come through. Explore our website to discover exciting opportunities, to connect, learn and serve in our church.

At Word Of Life Assembly Junior Church, we believe that every one has a unique purpose and role in our faith community. Our vision is to empower young individuals to discover their God-given gifts, cultivate their faith, and actively contribute to the church and society. We offer a variety of engaging programs tailored specifically for teenagers and children. There's something for everyone to explore and enjoy

OUR RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Study and Apply the Bible
  • Be Christ Ambassadors: Represent Christ
  • Sharing Our Faith: Preaching the Gospel
  • Using Social Media and Technology for Good
  • Praying for Others

OUR DUTIES:

  • Serving in the worship team or choir
  • Assisting in children's ministries
  • Volunteering in community outreach projects
  • Participating in youth leadership programs
  • Supporting fundraising initiatives for charitable causes
